Happy news from the Swedish [Vetenskapsradet](http://www.vr.se/) (via [Coturnix](http://scienceblogs.com/clock/2009/10/the_swedish_research_council_m.php)):
as of next 2010 all peer reviewed journal papers [must be Open Access](http://www.vr.se/franvetenskapsradet/nyheter/nyhetsarkiv/nyheter2009/nyheter2009/vetenskapsradetkraverfritillgangtillforskningsresultat.5.227c330c123c73dc586800011519.html).
I am not yet VR funded, but involved in a few VR grant applications. Not that that really matters, as I am happily
[publishing OA already](http://www.google.se/search?hl=sv&q=site:biomedcentral.com+willighagen&btnG=S%C3%B6k&meta=).
