[Mark](http://holtsblog.blogspot.com/2008/10/embedding-gist-in-your-blog.html) pointed me to the embed functionality of
[Gist](http://gist.github.com/), product on [GitHub <i class="fa-solid fa-recycle fa-xs"></i>](https://github.com/)
where I host [some todo software <i class="fa-solid fa-recycle fa-xs"></i>](https://chem-bla-ics.linkedchemistry.info/2008/10/20/gittodo-support-for-freemind-graphical.html)
and a [git mirror of CDK 1.2.x <i class="fa-solid fa-recycle fa-xs"></i>](https://chem-bla-ics.linkedchemistry.info/2008/09/30/git-mirror-for-cdk.html).

So, the other day, when I blogged about [Bioclipse2 scripts <i class="fa-solid fa-recycle fa-xs"></i>](https://chem-bla-ics.linkedchemistry.info/2008/10/25/bioclipse2-scripting-1-from-smiles-to.html),

<script src="https://gist.github.com/18315.js"></script>
