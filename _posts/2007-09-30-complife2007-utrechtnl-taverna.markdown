Two working days left before I'm off to two conferences. First, next Thursday/Friday, the two day [CompLife2007](http://www.inf.uni-konstanz.de/complife07/)
in Utrecht/NL, with sessions on genomics, systems biology, medical information and data analysis. And, on the second day tutorials on
[KNIME](http://knime.org/) and [CDK](http://cdk.sf.net/)/[Bioclipse](http://www.bioclipse.net/). I will try to orient as much as possible around
MS-based metabolomics, and metabolite identity in particular. [Last year](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/2006/09/complife06-day-1.html)
the conference was very interesting.

The Monday/Tuesday after that, I will present CDK-[Taverna](http://taverna.sourceforge.net/) integration I worked on in 2005 (see e.g.
[Taverna on Classpath](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/2006/05/taverna-runs-with-classpath-091.html) and
[CDK-Taverna fully recognized](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/2005/10/cdk-taverna-fully-recognized.html)) at the
[Taverna meeting](http://taverna.sourceforge.net/index.php?doc=workshop.html), before Thomas continued on that,
leading to the [cdk-taverna.de](http://cdk-taverna.de/) plugin website. If time permits, I will prepare an example
workflow from metabolomics. Unlike previous times I went to Cambridgeshire, I won't fly in on Stansted, but take the
[EuroStar](http://www.eurostar.com/) instead. I am very much looking forward to that. Unfortunately, I will not have time
to visit Cambridge itself, this time :(
