The Dutch company [PANalytical](http://www.panalytical.com/) has made their [HighScore software](http://www.panalytical.com/index.cfm?pargetinit=1&menuinit=1&pid=1324)
available (some details in this [README](http://www.crystallography.net/archives/2010/PANalytical/README.txt)) for use in the
[Crystallography Open Database](http://www.crystallography.net/).

[ICDD](http://www.icdd.com/) is rumored not to be amused by the contribution of the HighScore-based search functionality, and
rumored to be claiming breach of intellectual property. I have not seen either any ICDD patents nor the HighScore implementation,
but clearly there is a conflict of interest.

BTW, [Panton Principle](http://chem-bla-ics.blogspot.com/2010/02/open-data-panton-principles.html) endorsers may be
interesting in signing the [petition for Open Data in crystallograph](http://www.crystallography.net/petition/) too.
