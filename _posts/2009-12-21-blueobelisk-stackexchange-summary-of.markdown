The [Blue Obelisk StackExchange](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/) (BO<sub>x</sub>) has seen a relatively good start,
but the number of questions is dropping. The average number of unique visits is about 23-30 now:

![](/blog/assets/images/box.png)

The number of [registered users](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/users) is not insignificant but also
has not been growing much lately:

![](/blog/assets/images/box1.png)

At the same time, the quality of the questions are high, and have real users questions:

* [Good way to produce a table of R-groups](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/questions/86/good-way-to-produce-a-table-of-r-groups) (yet unanswered!)
* [Is there an open source pKa or LogD tool available](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/questions/91/is-there-an-open-source-pka-or-logd-tool-available)
* [Easiest way to align two molecules from the command line?](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/questions/81/easiest-way-to-align-two-molecules-from-the-command-line)

The overall state is [37 questions](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/questions) with about
[50 different tags](http://blueobelisk.stackexchange.com/tags):

![](/blog/assets/images/box2.png)

To the make progress with BO<sub>x</sub>, we primarily need to promote it more as central point of entry to people who
want to know what *free* tools they can use to perform there need, and to the people who want to contribute to
*ODOSOS cheminformatics*, by pointing out the unsolved problems.
